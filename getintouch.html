ABOUT ME

RESUME WRITING FOR ALL CAREER PATHS.

Hi, My Name is Kendra Moore and here is a little about me I have been helping job seekers obtain employment and advance their careers since 2018. I began as an intern resume writer and job search assistant at Irving Street Library in Westminster, Colorado. After graduating from Naropa University in 2019 with a B.A. in Contemplative Psychology, I worked as a construction recruiter before branching out into the behavioral health and workforce training industries. I started my own resume writing business, Resumes by Kendra, in 2020.

I have written hundreds of resumes in dozens of fields. Some of my specialties include:

  • CONSTRUCTION / SKILLED TRADES
  • RETAIL
  • ADMINISTRATION
  • SECURITY
  • STUDENTS / RECENT GRADS
  • ALTERNATIVE CAREER PATHS
